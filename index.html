MISSION DIRECTIVE

Semiconductors, robotics, and space technology form a single frontier technology stack. The same chips that power autonomous robots power satellite constellations. The same automation that assembles cars assembles rockets. Tracking these sectors separately misses the dependency chain that determines which systems scale, which bottleneck, and where value concentrates.

Robotnik maps the full stack — from the robot on the factory floor, to the satellite in orbit, down through their shared sensors, compute modules, chip designers, packaging houses, and foundries — because the people making the most expensive decisions in this space need to see the whole chain, not fragments of it.

This is the intelligence layer for the frontier technology stack. One index. One universe. One dependency chain.
