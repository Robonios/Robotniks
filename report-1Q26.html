Key findings

  • The Robotnik Composite returned +11.6% in Q1 2026, outperforming the S&P 500 (−4.6%), NASDAQ (−6.0%), and ROBO Global (−1.3%).
  • Three dependency chains drove the quarter: the rare earth supply shock, semiconductor reshoring, and the AI-to-physical deployment wave.
  • The Materials & Inputs sub-index returned +26.3%, the strongest of the four sub-indices.
  • $46bn was deployed across 91 private funding rounds, with robotics attracting roughly two-thirds of that capital.
  • Four of the top five Q1 performers sit at identified chokepoints in the frontier dependency chain.
